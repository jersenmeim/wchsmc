About Us

WCHSMC Consulting is spearheaded by an experienced and knowledgeable team with the goal of taking OHSE to greater heights in the pacific island. An accomplished, multifaceted, and resolutions focused leader with more than 18- years' experience as an occupational safety management specialist for an industry-leading organization. Supports organizations to elevate and integrate safety as a strategic business priority by providing guidance and direction to ensure that those involved in its operations do so in a safe, healthy, responsible, and acceptable manner. In depth knowledge of developing and executing health and safety plans & agreements, in line with local and international legal guidelines. Collaborates with executives to ensure that leadership “buy- in” and ownership for safety engagements have a ripple effect in the organization. Facilitates organizational safety risk assessments to evaluate, assess and recommend best practice to enable organizations to synthesize and integrate results to eliminate or minimize safety risks. Possesses a key strength in defining robust processes to ensure the utmost integrity of safety data.

Corporate Philosophy

The company was established to enable companies to break the OHSE plateau to greater heights; support companies that are at OHSE implementation stages; to support the Executives on Visible leadership and strengthen the capacity of OHSE Professionals to support business. Respond to business identified gaps that hinder them achieving or maintaining greater and sustained OHSE performance. To ensure client satisfaction with the delivery of integrated and sustainable standards in Occupational Health, Safety services. To facilitate and support organizations to take OHSE to greater heights.

Our success is based on fostering and developing long-term mutually beneficial professional relationships with our clients and our professional staff by investing in the resources that will maintain our position as the regional leader and provider of Occupational Health, safety and Environment consulting services, with nationally and internationally recognized expertise

Core Business

Consultation and management in a broad spectrum of Occupational Health, safety and Environment services and issues as they affect facilities , operations, and the people within the working environment.

Vision

The vision of the WCHSMC Consulting is to contribute in the achievement and maintenance of Zero Harm to people and environment at work by influencing efficiency of business operations through integration of OHSE processes in day to day and strategic activities using the consulting services to support, advise, coach, train and monito.

Mission

To provide a world class quality training and education to the organization and individuals that will enhance their competitiveness and competences in the field of safety internationally and to provide people with skills, professionalism, and opportunities to build a better future.
